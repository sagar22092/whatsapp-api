📖 How It Works

1. Create Session

Click "New Session" to create a WhatsApp session. It will appear instantly in your session list with status 'CREATED'.

2. Connect via QR

Click "Connect" to generate a QR code. Scan it with WhatsApp mobile app to link your account instantly.

3. Manage & Send Messages

After connecting, send messages, view chats, check account info, or logout sessions—all in real-time.
